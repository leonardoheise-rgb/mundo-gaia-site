# Mundo Gaia Growth Plan

## Objective

Turn the current site into a clearer commercial experience without losing the warm, human, and non-pushy tone that already makes Mundo Gaia distinct.

The main opportunity is not to sound more polished. It is to make the offer easier to understand, easier to trust, and easier to act on.

## What Is Already Strong

- Warm and differentiated brand tone
- Real founder story with credible background
- Audio preview on the home page
- Helpful FAQ structure
- SEO-oriented benefit pages and blog content
- Good alignment between product promise and audience pain points

## Main Gaps To Fix

1. The site explains the feeling of the app better than the product itself.
2. The site asks people to download before fully answering what they get.
3. Trust is present, but it is not packaged strongly enough near decision points.
4. There is only one main conversion path: download the app.
5. The main pages are empathetic, but not segmented enough for different visitor intents.

## Strategy

Keep the emotional positioning and Deliane's authority, but make the site more concrete in three areas:

- Marketing: clearer message and stronger differentiation
- Sales: lower friction and stronger reasons to install now
- Customer relationship: more support before and after download

## Phase 1: Quick Wins

These changes should have the highest impact with the lowest implementation effort.

### 1. Clarify "what is inside the app"

Add a section on the home page that explains:

- Types of content available
- Age range the app serves
- Typical moments of use
- What the parent experiences on day one

Suggested content blocks:

- Histórias para dormir
- Meditações guiadas
- Áudios para transições e calma
- Conteúdos para emoções intensas
- Rituais curtos para a rotina noturna

### 2. Strengthen trust near the hero and CTA

Add trust elements close to the first download buttons:

- Founder credentials in one short line
- Number of testimonials or families if available
- App store rating if available
- "Criado por humanos" message kept, but made shorter and sharper

Suggested direction:

`Criado por Deliane, educadora física, instrutora de Yoga e Meditação e pós-graduada em Neurociências do Comportamento e ABA.`

### 3. Explain the offer with less ambiguity

Parents should not need to infer the business model.

Add a compact explanation near download CTAs:

- Free to download
- What is available immediately
- Whether there is premium content or a subscription
- Which platforms are supported

If there is a subscription, make it gentle and transparent instead of hiding it.

### 4. Create one softer conversion path

Add a lead capture option for people who are interested but not ready to install.

Best options:

- Free bedtime routine guide
- Printable calming activity
- Short email sequence: "7 noites mais tranquilas"

This creates a relationship before the app install.

### 5. Upgrade the testimonial block

Keep the emotional tone, but make the section more commercially useful.

Improve it with:

- More testimonials
- Labels by use case: sono, vínculo, gestação, criança agitada
- Optional app review screenshots if available
- One short quote from a professional or specialist if available

## Phase 2: Homepage Restructure

Reorganize the home page so that it answers the user's main questions in the right order.

### Recommended order

1. Hero
2. Trust bar
3. What the app is
4. What is inside the app
5. Audio preview
6. Who it is for
7. Specific use cases
8. Deliane and authority
9. Testimonials
10. FAQ
11. Final CTA

### Hero rewrite direction

The current hero is warm and good, but it can be more concrete.

It should answer:

- Who it is for
- What the app does
- When it helps
- Why it is different

Suggested angle:

`Histórias e meditações guiadas para ajudar crianças a desacelerar, dormir com mais calma e viver a rotina com mais acolhimento.`

### New section: "What makes Mundo Gaia different"

Add a comparison-style block without naming competitors.

Possible differentiators:

- Conteúdo criado por humanos
- Voz acolhedora e sem excesso de estímulo
- Baseado na experiência real da Deliane com infância, corpo e emoções
- Feito para a rotina real, não para uma rotina idealizada
- Une história, meditação e vínculo em vez de apenas distração

## Phase 3: Better Sales Journey

### 1. Add intent-based entry points

Create clearer pathways from the home page to different user intents:

- Para dormir melhor
- Para acalmar criança agitada
- Para reconhecer emoções
- Para fortalecer vínculo
- Para famílias que querem uma rotina mais leve

These can link to existing benefits pages and blog posts.

### 2. Improve CTA language by page context

Avoid using the same CTA everywhere.

Suggested CTA variations:

- `Ouvir uma prévia real`
- `Ver como funciona`
- `Baixar o app`
- `Criar uma rotina mais calma`
- `Explorar histórias e meditações`

### 3. Add a "Why install now" section

Competitors do this better by creating urgency without sounding aggressive.

Suggested approach:

- Teste hoje à noite com sua criança
- Escute uma prévia antes de decidir
- Comece com um ritual simples de poucos minutos

This keeps your gentle tone while still creating momentum.

## Phase 4: Customer-Facing Relationship

### 1. Expand support resources

Build more visible pre-download and post-download support:

- Guides for bedtime routine
- Articles for common scenarios
- Printable breathing activities
- A "start here" page for new families

### 2. Add scenario-based guidance

Make the main pages more specific about when the app helps:

- Noites agitadas
- Dificuldade para desacelerar
- Emoções intensas no fim do dia
- Medo do escuro
- Choro antes de dormir
- Transições e previsibilidade

This will make parents feel more understood.

### 3. Build more visible authority around Deliane

The current founder page is good. The next step is to distribute Deliane's authority throughout the site.

Add:

- Short expert signature under key sections
- "Orientado por Deliane" blocks in blog and benefits pages
- A small credentials ribbon
- More first-person guidance where it makes sense

## Phase 5: Content and SEO With Better Conversion

The site already has a strong blog direction. The next improvement is connecting content to conversion more deliberately.

### Priority content clusters

- História para dormir
- Meditação para crianças
- Acalmar criança agitada
- Rotina noturna infantil
- Respiração para crianças
- Emoções na infância

### Conversion improvements for blog posts

Each article should include:

- A short expert intro with Deliane
- A contextual CTA, not only "baixar o app"
- Related articles by problem type
- A next-step suggestion for the reader

Suggested next-step CTAs:

- `Ouça uma prévia do app`
- `Veja como o app ajuda na rotina noturna`
- `Explore histórias para dormir`

## Measurement Plan

Track the impact of the changes with a few simple metrics.

### Top metrics

- Click rate on App Store button
- Click rate on Google Play button
- Audio preview plays
- Scroll depth on home page
- CTR from blog posts to app download
- Email capture rate if lead magnet is added

### Questions to answer with data

- Do visitors understand the offer faster?
- Does trust content increase download clicks?
- Does a softer conversion path recover visitors who are not ready yet?
- Which use cases drive the most app interest?

## Suggested Execution Order

1. Rewrite the home page structure and messaging
2. Add a concrete "what is inside the app" section
3. Strengthen trust near the hero and first CTA
4. Improve testimonials and proof packaging
5. Add a softer conversion asset
6. Refine benefit pages by user intent
7. Improve blog CTAs and internal linking
8. Add measurement checkpoints

## Recommended Next Deliverables

If this plan is approved, the next assets to produce should be:

1. A new home page wireframe in markdown
2. A section-by-section copy plan for the home page
3. Revised CTA language for each page type
4. A lead magnet concept and capture flow
5. A trust-proof checklist for testimonials, ratings, and credentials

## Final Direction

Mundo Gaia does not need to copy U.S. competitors.

The best move is to keep the emotional softness and human presence that already make the brand special, while adopting stronger commercial structure:

- more clarity
- more trust
- more segmentation
- more conversion paths
- more proof near the moment of decision

That combination should make the site feel both more caring and more effective.
