#prompt.md

Create a website for the brazilian app Mundo Gaia. The domain will be "corujagaia.com.br". It must be fully in Portuguese. 

SITE DESIGN
Design a modern, calming, emotionally intelligent website inspired by the aesthetic and UX philosophy of How We Feel and Headspace. It must be minimalistic. Minimalist and soft UI, Rounded elements and friendly typography, High accessibility (large buttons, high contrast), Mobile-first responsive design, Smooth micro-interactions and transitions. You can create svg icons if necessary.

- alignment: "Centralizado ou alinhado à esquerda com bastante respiro"

. Tone of Voice
Calm and Nurturing
Speaks with serenity, like someone offering a safe embrace. Uses short, measured sentences.
Affectionate and Empathetic
Recognizes the challenges of everyday life with children and welcomes parents' feelings.
Playful and Magical
Inspires imagination, but without infantilizing the adult. Uses mental imagery that evokes nature, gentle fantasy, and symbolic elements.
Gentle and Confident
Shows knowledge with lightness. Avoids jargon or overly technical terms. Speaks to parents as equals, but with softness.
Clarity Without Infantilization
Even within a child-centered universe, communication is written in adult, simple, and respectful language.

4. What to avoid
* An authoritative or imposing tone ("do this," "you need to use this")
* Excessive infantilization when speaking to parents ("your little baby bird will love it")
* Overly technical language ("mindfulness," "neuroplasticity")
* Exaggerated urgency ("don't miss it!", "today only!")
________________


5. Adaptation by channel
* Short and impactful sentences, with enchanting visual elements.
* Dont use emojis or use it very cautiously only if really necessary or if it really makes sense
* Objective guidance, but always with gentle language.
* Short sentences, with room to breathe. Slightly more explanatory content.
* Stories and situations parents can relate to.
